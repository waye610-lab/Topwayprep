Planning Your Time 合理安排時間

Take a breath — you’ve prepared for this. A few gentle reminders to help you do your best:

  • With the timer on, you’ll have 180 minutes — the same as the real test. Think of it as a chance to get comfortable with the pace.
  • This practice mirrors the computer-adaptive test (CAT): for Math and stand-alone revising & editing questions, you can’t go back once you move on, so settle on your answer before advancing.
  • Within a single reading or revising & editing passage set, you can return and change answers — but once you leave the last question in that set, it locks.
  • Don’t dwell too long on any one question. Trust your preparation — when you’re unsure, choose your best answer and keep moving forward.
  • Finish the subject you started before moving to the next. Once you submit a subject area, you won’t be able to return to it.
